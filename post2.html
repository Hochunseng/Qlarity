What skin concern do you need help with?


Select which skin concern(s) you are addressing:

#acne

#acne

×
Acne is a skin condition that occurs when hair follicles plug with oil and dead skin cells. Symptoms range from uninflamed blackheads to pus-filled pimples or large, red, and tender bumps.
#anti-aging

#anti-aging

×
Anti-aging products and routines make the user look younger by reducing, masking or preventing signs of skin aging. These signs may include wrinkles, fine lines, and loss of elasticity.
#combo

#combo

×
Combination skin is simply having oily skin in some areas of your face and dry skin in other areas. Typically, there's a mix of oily and dry areas on different parts of your face, with the t-zone (forehead, nose, and chin) being slightly to very oily.
#dryness

#dryness

×
Dry skin is an uncomfortable condition marked by scaling, itching, and cracking. Even if your skin tends to be oily, you can develop dry skin from time to time. Dry skin can affect any part of your body, but most commonly affects hands, arms, and legs.
#normal

#normal

×
Normal skin has a good balance of moisture, small pores and an even tone, and is the goal of most skin care regimens.

#oily

#oily

×
Oily skin is the result of the overproduction of sebum from sebaceous glands. Sebum helps protect and moisturize your skin and keeps your hair shiny and healthy. Too much sebum, however, may lead to oily skin, which can then lead to clogged pores and acne.
#pigmentation

#pigmentation

×
Hyperpigmentation is a common, usually harmless condition in which patches of skin become darker in color than the normal surrounding skin. It is also known as sun spots, age spots, dark spots, or post-inflammatory hyperpigmentation, which is a result of the inflammation acne can cause.
#redness

#redness

×
Perhaps the most widely documented cause of redness is rosacea, a condition that causes redness and often small, red, pus-filled bumps on the face. Sensitive skin can also be prone to redness if it comes into contact with irritating ingredients.
#sensitive

#sensitive

×
Sensitive skin is prone to itching and irritation when coming into contact with certain cosmetics and skincare products.
